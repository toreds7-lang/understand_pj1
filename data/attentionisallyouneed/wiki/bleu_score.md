# BLEU Score
_Type: metric | Papers: attentionisallyouneed_

## Summary
BLEU Score, or Bilingual Evaluation Understudy Score, is a metric for evaluating the quality of text which has been machine-translated from one language to another. It assesses the correspondence between a machine's output and that of a human by counting matching n-grams. High BLEU scores indicate high quality, making it significant in machine translation and natural language processing tasks to objectively measure model performance.
