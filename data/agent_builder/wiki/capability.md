# Capability
_Type: concept | Papers: agent_builder_

## Summary
Capability refers to the set of skills or competencies that an entity, such as a machine learning model or software agent, possesses and can effectively employ to perform specific tasks. In the context of machine learning, capability often denotes the ability of a model to generalize from training data to unseen situations or tasks. This concept is significant because it determines the practicality, adaptability, and overall success of models in real-world applications.
