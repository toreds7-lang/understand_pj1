# Generative AI Models
_Type: concept | Papers: agent_builder_

## Summary
Generative AI Models are a subset of artificial intelligence designed to generate new data, such as images, text, or music, that mimic existing content. These models are significant for their ability to assist in creativity, simulate potential scenarios in simulations, and automate content creation, impacting industries from entertainment to pharmaceuticals.
