## Summary
The Requirements Elicitation Study is a systematic method used in software engineering to gather and define user requirements early in the development process. Its significance lies in reducing misunderstandings and ensuring that the final product meets the user's needs and expectations. This method often involves users and stakeholders actively, which can lead to more accurate and efficient development cycles.
