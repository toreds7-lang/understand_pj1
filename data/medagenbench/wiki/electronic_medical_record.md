# Electronic Medical Record
_Type: concept | Papers: medagenbench_

## Summary
An Electronic Medical Record (EMR) is a digital version of a patient’s paper chart. EMRs are real-time, patient-centered records that make information available instantly and securely to authorized users. They are significant in modern healthcare because they facilitate the efficient storage, retrieval, and sharing of patient information, thereby improving the quality of patient care.
