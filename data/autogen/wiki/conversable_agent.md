# Conversable Agent
_Type: concept | Papers: autogen_

## Summary
A conversable agent is an artificial intelligence entity designed to interact with users or other systems through natural language dialogue. Utilizing underlying Large Language Models (LLMs), these agents can engage in meaningful, context-aware conversations, greatly enhancing user experience and functionality in various applications. The significance of conversable agents lies in their ability to provide intuitive and personalized interactions, making them essential in areas such as customer service, personal assistants, and autonomous multi-agent systems.
