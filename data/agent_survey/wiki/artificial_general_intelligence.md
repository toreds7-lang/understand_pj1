## Summary
Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) refers to a type of artificial intelligence that possesses the ability to understand, learn, and apply intelligence in a manner that is indistinguishable from that of humans across a wide range of tasks. Its significance lies in the potential to revolutionize industries, solve complex global challenges, and transform the way humans interact with technology, achieving a level of versatile problem-solving currently unmatched by narrow AI systems.
