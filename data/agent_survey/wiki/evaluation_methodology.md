## Summary
Evaluation methodology refers to the systematic approach used to assess the performance, effectiveness, and impact of a given model, algorithm, or system. It is significant in machine learning and computer science as it provides a framework for comparing different systems using standardized criteria and benchmarks, ensuring reliability and generalizability of the findings.
