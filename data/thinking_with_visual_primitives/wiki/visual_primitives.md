## Summary
Visual primitives are foundational elements such as points and bounding boxes that are used as basic building blocks in computer vision and machine learning algorithms. These primitives play a crucial role in efficiently processing and understanding visual data by defining spatial relationships and regions of interest within images, aiding in object detection and image annotation tasks.
