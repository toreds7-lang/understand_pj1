# Perception Gap
_Type: concept | Papers: thinking_with_visual_primitives_

## Summary
The perception gap refers to the difference between how a machine learning model perceives and interprets visual data compared to human perception. It is significant because understanding and narrowing this gap is crucial for developing models that can perform tasks requiring nuanced interpretation of visual information, similarly to humans. This concept is essential in the realm of artificial intelligence to ensure models respond to visual stimuli in ways that align more closely with human understanding.
